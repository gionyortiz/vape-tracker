Quick troubleshooting

  • Use a modern browser (Edge/Chrome) and load the app via HTTPS or localhost.
  • To reset the app, clear browser storage or use the in‑app Data Manager to export and re‑import.
  • For camera/barcode access, ensure you’ve granted permissions to the site.
  • Offline mode works after the first load; hard‑refresh if updates don’t appear.

Data exports

Open the Data Manager inside the app to export inventory, transactions, and customers. Keep backups before clearing data.
